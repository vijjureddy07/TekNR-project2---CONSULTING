# Page Structure

## Public Pages
- `index.html`: primary homepage
- `home-page-2.html`: alternate homepage
- `about.html`: authority and brand story
- `services.html` / `services-details.html`: consulting offers
- `courses.html` / `courses-details.html`: digital products
- `case-studies.html` / `case-study-details.html`: proof pages
- `blog.html` / `blog-details.html`: content and SEO pages
- `contact-us.html`: contact and booking entry
- `pricing.html`: offer comparison

## Utility Pages
- `checkout.html`, `thank-you.html`, `404.html`, `coming-soon.html`, `privacy-policy.html`, `terms-and-conditions.html`

## Auth + Dashboard
- auth pages under `pages/`
- client/admin dashboards under `pages/dashboard/`
